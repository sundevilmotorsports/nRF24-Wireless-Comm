##Pinout for Arduino Leonardo
RF Receiver ----> Leonardo
CE ----> 1 (TX1)
CS ----> 10
SCK ----> ICSP
SDO ----> ICSP
SDI ----> ICSP
GND
3.3V


##Pinout for Teensy

CS ----> 37
SCK ----> 13
SDO ----> 12
SDI ----> 11
GND
3.3V
